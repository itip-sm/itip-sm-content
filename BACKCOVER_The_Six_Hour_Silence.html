A father drives six hours to watch his fourteen-year-old daughter play a tournament match. She loses badly. He opens his mouth and says exactly the wrong thing. She puts in her headphones, turns to the window, and disappears.

For four hours, they sit side by side in total silence — two people who love each other, separated by everything left unsaid. Then, at a service station on a Spanish highway, he makes a decision that changes their relationship forever. Not a speech. Not a lecture. Something far smaller — and infinitely braver.
